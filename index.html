about.

Welcome to my portfolio! If you made it this far then yay. As it says, I’m Mariah. I am passionate about design and am dedicated to be the best I can be. When I’m not at school or work I love to travel and pursue my interest in photography. I was born and raised in Spokane, Washington but was lucky enough to land a job and escape to the big apple for six months. I moved to New York during a gap between classes and worked at a coffee shop in Manhattan while living in a studio in Brooklyn. Let me tell you, working in a coffee shop in Manhattan was INSANE, but I loved it.

Most, if not all of my inspiration and motivation comes from hard work and traveling. Living in New York was such an experience and design was everywhere- from the clothes, to the buildings, to the people. It was amazing.I am a very motivated and funny person. I like to laugh-you can’t take life too seriously. I have worked since I was 15 and have sent myself around the US and to Europe and I’m really proud of it. I have gained knowledge on different styles of design around the globe. These experiences have helped me grow as a person and as a designer.
